The PatternRipple seamless pattern checker is a free browser tool for previewing repeat tiles. Upload a PNG or JPG or paste an image URL and view a 4x4 grid with optional gaps to check edges. Designers use it to vet fabric and wallpaper repeats before buying or exporting finals. Explore related designs in the Faux Embroidery, Abstract, and Geometric collections.
